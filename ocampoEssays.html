OCAMPO ESSAYS

WINTER 2013

For Sophomore year my teacher, Mr. Ocampo, gave us an essay after we finished reading a novel. We went through Fahrenheit 451, Outliers, Siddhartha Gautama, and more throughout the year. Each essay we had the option to become partnered with someone in our class and I always took that option because I thought it would be interesting to see the writing styles of someone else.

I would say that every essay helped me progress as a writer. I started the year off with, if I recall correctly, a C- on his papers. Afterwards I started to receive better grades essay after essay. Partnering up with someone helped me with my editing as well. My partner and I would look over each other’s work to see our errors and correct it. It was pretty effective considering that I never went to anyone else for advice prior to these assignments.
