MODES PROJECT

WINTER 2013

For AP English my instructor, Mrs. Bailin, assigned us a project where we wrote several essays and academic summaries (precis) based on the nine modes of literature, narration, description, exemplification, process, cause/effect, compare/contrast, classification/division, definition, and argument. We were also put into a team of two so we can analyze the mode that we were assigned. I was assigned the exemplification mode, which is essentially an essay where you provide a series of examples as proof for an argument. We presented this to the class along with providing handouts that pertained to our mode of literature.

The project was time consuming, occupying most of my free time. An essay and a preci was due every week and it was an arduous task to complete both, along with completing other deadlines I had throughout the week. I would say that the preci writing made my writing stronger! It helped me with incorporating source text into my own writing and it allowed for my analysis skills to grow. The essays, while not the best, were a good exercise to help me get accustomed to writing essays every week. I am capable of writing essays with ease now. Prior to this project I had difficulty beginning a paragraph, but it comes naturally now.
