NHS: HALLOWEEN GIFTS

FALL 2013

My school's NHS chapter does an annual event where we make gift bags for the local Shadetree. The Shadetree is a nonprofit organization that shelters abused women and their children. My officer team and I thought that we could give back to the community by delivering goodie bags full of candy for the children considering that they do not get to leave their shelter to trick or treat.

This impacted me on a level I can't even begin to describe. Outside the shelter I saw a block lined with homeless people shivering out in the cold. The children in the shelter were my age and they had to live with scant resources. It gave me a new appreciation of life and I will never forget it. This qualifies for my school's teamwork/collaboration tenant because it required me to work in coordination with my officer team.
