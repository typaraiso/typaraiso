LEAD CONFERENCE: DENVER

FALL 2013

The annual Lead conference is for NHS and STUCO chapters around the nation. Students that are a part of these chapters congregate in one location every year to develop their leadership abilities and build connections with like minded people. My chapter's officer team went, and it was a requirement to attend because I am my NHS chapter's president elect.

The conference was great and I participated in a leadership building workshop. I worked in coordination with other students to build my abilities to lead others. The conference was fantastic because of the guest speakers they chose to speak for us. I will always remember the words of encouragement given to me to always persevere no matter the circumstance. This qualifies for my school's teamwork/collaboration tenant because it built my skills to work in a team.
