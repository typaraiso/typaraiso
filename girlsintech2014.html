GIRLS IN TECH: 2014

SPRING 2014

The Girls in Tech conference is conducted on an annual basis by the Web Design & Development and Video Game Design program areas at Southwest Career Technical Academy. The purpose of this conference is to increase awareness about the women in the technology field and provide an avenue for middle school girls to develop their abilities.

I was a part of the web design team this year. The website that was designed for this conference can be found here: http://www.swcta.net/teachers/changcl/IT2014/
